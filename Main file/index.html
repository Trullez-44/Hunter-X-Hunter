STORY WORLD NEN CHARACTERS

STORY

Twelve years before the start of the story, Ging Freecss abandoned his son Gon and left him in the care of his cousin Mito on Whale Island. As Gon grows up, he discovers that his father (who was supposedly dead) had abandoned him to become one of the best Hunters. Motivated by this, Gon decides to leave his home and apply for the Hunter Exam, which consists of overcoming a series of challenges that seek to test the skills, survival, and teamwork of its participants. During the exam, Gon meets and befriends three other participants: Kurapika, the last member of the Kurta Clan, who wishes to become a Hunter in order to avenge his family and recover the scarlet eyes that were stolen from their bodies by a group of mercenaries called Gen’ei Ryodan; Leorio, who just wants to be a Hunter so he can pay for his medical studies; and Killua, a young man who left his previous life as a member of the most famous family of assassins. Thus, Gon in the company of his friends will live a series of adventures while each tries to fulfill their wish.

WORLD

Hunters are licensed, elite members of humanity who are capable of tracking down secret treasures, rare beasts, or even other individuals. They can also access locations that regulars cannot access. To obtain a Hunter License one must pass the rigorous annual Hunter Exam run by the Hunter Association, which has a success rate of less than one in a hundred-thousand. A Hunter may be awarded up to three stars; a single star for making "remarkable achievements in a particular field"; they may then be upgraded to two stars for "holding an official position" and mentoring another Hunter up to single star level; and finally upgraded to three stars for "remarkable achievements in multiple fields.

GEOGRAPHY

The fictional world of Hunter × Hunter is composed of a large ocean and 6 continents that are actually those of our world only with a different order, with South America in the north and inverted. So far, only the names of 2 continents are known: The Yorbian Continent and the Azian Continent. There are 250 countries in the world. In reality, the “ocean” that surrounds the continents is just a large lake called Mobius on the Dark Continent, which is the unknown world, beyond the borders.

NEN

Nen is the ability to control one's own life energy or aura, which is constantly emitted from them whether they know it or not. There are four basic Nen techniques; Ten maintains the aura in the body, strengthening it for defense, Zetsu shuts the aura flow off, useful for concealing one's presence and relieving fatigue, Ren enables a user to produce more Nen, and Hatsu is a person's specific use of Nen. Nen users are classified into six types based on their Hatsu abilities; Enhancers strengthen and reinforce their natural physical abilities, Emitters project aura out of their bodies,Manipulators control objects or living things, Transmuters change the type or properties of their aura, Conjurers create objects out of their aura, and Specialists have unique abilities that do not fall into the previous categories. A Nen user can enter into a Contract whereby pledging to follow certain Limitations, their abilities are strengthened in relation to how strict they are. An example of this is Kurapika who, in order to have an unbreakable chain that will hold members of the Phantom Troupe no matter what, offered his life should he use it on anyone other than its members.

MAIN CHARACTERS

GON FREECSS

Introduction

Gon Freecss is a twelve-year-old boy from Whale Island who dreams of following in his father’s footsteps and becoming a renowned hunter, his goal is to find him. He spent much of his childhood in nature, which allowed him to develop a special affinity and skills for interacting with animals. In addition, his sharp senses, mainly his sense of smell and sight, are exceptional. His best friend is Killua Zoldick.

Nen

Gon is an enhancer, which gives him great strength and resilience.

KILLUA ZOLDICK

Introduction

Killua Zoldick is a twelve-year-old boy and best friend of the protagonist, Gon Freecss. Killua Zoldyck belongs to a famous family of assassins, the Zoldyck family. Avoiding having the same future, he decided to participate in the hunter exam where we are later shown the great friendship he forms with our protagonist. He is very agile and strong in combat. He has a quite cheerful and serene attitude, in moments of danger he tends to be cold and calculating.

Nen

As a transmuter, Killua Zoldyck is more effective in changing the shape of his Nen. Therefore, Killua developed a Hatsu in which he transforms his aura into electricity.

KURAPIKA

Introduction

Kurapika is the last member of the Kuruta clan, known for their scarlet eyes that glow in a beautiful and coveted scarlet when experiencing intense emotions. His goal is to pursue and destroy the group responsible for the destruction of his clan, the Gen’ei Ryodan.

Nen

Kurapika is a Materializer, but when using his scarlet eyes he becomes a Specialist, with the ability to use all branches of Nen to their full potential.

Leorio Paradinight

Introduction

Leorio is a teenager who initially comments on participating in the hunter exam exclusively for money, but later reveals that it is because he wants to become a doctor and needs the money to pay for his studies. Leorio is a very intelligent person, willing to help his friends no matter what happens.

Nen

Leorio is an emitter. At the time he met Gon and Killua in Yorknew City, he could only use Ten and had no knowledge of the existence of other techniques, he learned more about Nen from the two children.

MAIN ANTAGONIST

MERUEM

Introduction

Meruem was the most powerful offspring of the Chimera Ant Queen. He was known as the "King" of the Chimera Ants.

Nen

Meruem is an Emitter. He was born with the ability to use Nen and a vastly superhuman amount of aura, which exceeded even that of the Royal Guards. By feeding on Nen users, he increased the size of his aura even further. After consuming Shaiapouf and Menthuthuyoupi, his aura reserves and output became such that even the overly protective Royal Guards had to admit the King seemed invincible and capable of winning a Nen battle through the sheer quantity of one's aura.
